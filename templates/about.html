About

PredPaRNA Online Prediction System

The research on the function of paRNA in this field is still in its infancy, so in order to better study the function of paRNA, it is necessary to identify paRNA from non-coding RNA data first.However, paRNA sequence recognition is still a challenging task due to its easy degradation and high specificity.


PredPaRNA is a model constructed by one-dimensional convolutional neural network (1DCNN) and bidirectional long and short-term memory algorithm (BiLSTM).
